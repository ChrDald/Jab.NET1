Member Links (All)

If the selected part is a Group, this collection includes all Links whose parent is your selected Group, or whose parent is recursively a member group of your selected group.  For example, Group Chi has no immediate child member Links. However, Link D --> F is a member of Group Omega, which in turn is a member of Group Psi. Thus, clicking on Chi will result in Link D --> F being highlighted.

The logic to highlight the appropriate Links is in the RecursiveChildren method.  Each successive child relationship increases the Highlight property value.
